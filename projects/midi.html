MIDI Based Music Keypad

This was a very small project, but it was my first attempt at 3D printing. I was able to utilize a Tazbot printer from school at the time to print an enclosure for a MIDI launchpad. This is what most people call this device that has programable buttons that connect to a computer or other audio equipment. This eventually was used for playing different sound bytes during my friend's podcast episodes. It has volume control through a potentiometer as well as 16 programable buttons that map to many computer softwware through a MIDI protocol. I learned a lot about the MIDI protocol throught this process.
