Instructions:
This game mimics the rules of 2048. You may use either the on-screen buttons or the arrow keys on the keyboard to move. There are numbers corresponding to each of the images, and they are displayed by clicking the toggle button below. The numbers will show up in the bottom right corner of each picture to help. Closing or refreshing the page will restart the game. If the window is too small and there is a scroll bar, use F11 to go full screen, or click shrink photos. ENJOY!
